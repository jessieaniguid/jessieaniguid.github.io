Sound of Light

In this 2D platformer that explores the absence of light, the player must use Lyte's voice to navigate through ancient caverns and restore the world's light.

This game was made in Unity by me and three other people. I worked on most of the code, while the others created the art and sounds. This was made in six weeks.

The Hunt

In this 2D hunting game set in prehistoric times, the player is tasked with babysitting their friend, who in return promises future help.

This was the first game I made in Unity. I was responsible for the code, and my friend dealt with the art.
